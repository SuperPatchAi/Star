| Welcomes feedback (Q18) | +5 |

### Red Flags

| Flag | Points |
|------|--------|
| Revenue under $250K | -10 |
| Not sure about investment (Q9) | -15 |
| Commitment under 7 (Q21) | -10 |
| Less than 5 hours/week available | -10 |
| Finds feedback difficult (Q18) | -5 |

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 60+ | Fast-track to discovery call |
| 40-59 | Standard review, likely qualified |
| 25-39 | Review carefully, may not be ideal fit |
| Under 25 | Likely decline or defer |
