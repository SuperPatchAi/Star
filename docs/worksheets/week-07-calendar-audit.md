## Part 1: The 80/15/5 Rule

| Level | % of Time | Activity Type | Examples |
|-------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| **Level 1** | 80% | **ACTION** (Revenue-generating) | Sales calls, demos, closing, prospecting, delivery |
| **Level 2** | 15% | **NURTURE** (Relationship building) | Follow-ups, networking, check-ins, community |
| **Level 3** | 5% | **TRAINING** (Personal development) | Courses, books, podcasts, conferences |

**Key Insight:** Most struggling entrepreneurs have this INVERTED (80% training, 5% action).
