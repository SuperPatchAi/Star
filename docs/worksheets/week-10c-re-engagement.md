## Part 3: The 4-Email Re-Engagement Sequence

### Email 1: "We Miss You" (Day 45)
**Trigger:** No purchase/engagement in 45 days

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Purpose** | Re-establish connection, soft reminder |
| **Tone** | Warm, friendly, not pushy |
| **Offer** | 15% off next order |
| **CTA** | "Come back and save" |

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: We miss you, [Name]!

Hi [Name],

It's been a while since we've seen you, and we wanted to check in.

We noticed you haven't [ordered/logged in/visited] recently, 
and we hope everything is okay!

As a thank you for being part of our community, here's 15% off 
your next [order/subscription]:

Use code: COMEBACK15

[BUTTON: Shop Now]

If there's anything we can help with, just reply to this email.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

### Email 2: "Is Everything Okay?" (Day 60)
**Trigger:** Still no engagement after Email 1

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Purpose** | Personal check-in, understand why |
| **Tone** | Concerned, helpful |
| **Offer** | None (relationship focus) |
| **CTA** | "Reply and let us know" |

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: [Name], is everything okay?

Hi [Name],

I noticed you haven't been back in a while, and I wanted to 
personally reach out.

Was there something about your experience that didn't meet 
your expectations? Did life just get busy?

I'd genuinely love to know — your feedback helps us improve.

Just hit reply and let me know. I read every email personally.

[Your Name]
Founder/CEO, [Company]

P.S. If you're still interested but need some help, I'm here.
```

---

### Email 3: "Last Chance" (Day 75)
**Trigger:** Still no engagement after Email 2

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Purpose** | Create urgency, best offer |
| **Tone** | Urgent but respectful |
| **Offer** | 25% off (best offer) |
| **CTA** | "Claim your discount" |

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Your 25% discount expires soon, [Name]

Hi [Name],

This is my last attempt to win you back.

I've saved a special 25% discount just for you, but it expires 
in 72 hours.

Use code: LASTCHANCE25

[BUTTON: Claim Your Discount]

If you've moved on, I completely understand. But if there's 
even a small chance you'd like to give us another try, this 
is the best offer I can make.

Either way, thank you for being a customer.

[Your Name]
```

---

### Email 4: "Goodbye (For Now)" (Day 90)
**Trigger:** Still no engagement after Email 3

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Purpose** | Graceful exit, leave door open |
| **Tone** | Respectful, no hard sell |
| **Offer** | None (or link to reactivate later) |
| **CTA** | "Unsubscribe or reactivate anytime" |

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Goodbye for now, [Name]

Hi [Name],

Since we haven't heard from you, I'm going to stop sending 
you emails (nobody likes a cluttered inbox).

Your account will remain active, and you can come back 
anytime — we'll be here.

If you ever want to reconnect, just reply to this email or 
visit [link].

Thank you for the time you spent with us. It meant a lot.

Wishing you all the best,
[Your Name]

[Unsubscribe] | [Reactivate My Account]
```
