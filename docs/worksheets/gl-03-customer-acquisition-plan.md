## Part 3: Customer Retention Strategy

Acquiring a customer costs 5x more than keeping one. Retention is where profitability lives.

### SmartShip Follow-Up Cadence

| Touchpoint | Timing | Action | Completed |
|------------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Welcome** | Day 1 | Welcome message + product tips for their specific patch | ☐ |
| **Check-In** | Day 10 | "How's your experience? Any questions?" | ☐ |
| **Pre-Renewal** | Day 25 | Reminder that SmartShip renews soon + cross-sell suggestion | ☐ |
| **Renewal Confirmation** | Day 30 | Confirm renewal processed + thank you | ☐ |
| **Cross-Sell** | Day 45 | Personalized product recommendation based on usage | ☐ |

### Re-Engagement Plan for Lapsed Customers

| Touchpoint | Timing | Action | Method |
|------------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Soft Check-In** | Day 45 (after last order) | "Hey, haven't heard from you — how's everything?" | Text / DM |
| **Value Add** | Day 60 | Share a new testimonial or product update relevant to them | Email / Social |
| **Re-Offer** | Day 75 | Special incentive or bundle suggestion to restart | Call / Text |
| **Final Attempt** | Day 90 | Direct conversation: "Is there anything I can help with?" | Phone call |
