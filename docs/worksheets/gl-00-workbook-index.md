## Overview

Welcome to the **Super Patch Global Leaders 2026 Planning Workbook** — your strategic companion for the leadership breakout session with CEO Jay Dhaliwal.

This workbook is designed to help you move beyond inspiration and into execution. Through a series of structured worksheets, you will clarify your 2026 vision, set measurable targets aligned to the Super Patch compensation plan, build recruiting and customer acquisition action plans, and commit to the daily activities that drive rank advancement.

**This is not a passive exercise.** Each worksheet requires honest self-assessment, specific numbers, and written commitments. The leaders who complete every section leave this workshop with a 12-month blueprint — not just motivation.

## How to Use This Workbook

1. **Complete worksheets in order.** Each one builds on the previous — your vision drives your recruiting plan, and your recruiting plan feeds your customer strategy.
2. **Use real numbers.** Pull your actual rank, TGQV, team size, and income from your Super Patch back office before the workshop.
3. **Be specific.** "Grow my team" is not a goal. "Enroll 5 associates per month at the PanAm kit level, with 80% SmartShip attachment" is a goal.
4. **Sign every commitment statement.** Written commitments outperform unwritten intentions by 42% (Dominican University study).
5. **Bring this workbook to every accountability check-in.** It is your operating plan for 2026.
