### Meeting Format Templates

**Team Call Format (30 minutes)**

| Segment | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Recognition | 5 min | Shout-outs for rank advances, Hat Tricks, All-Stars, Driver bonuses |
| Training | 10 min | One focused skill — demos/samples, 3-way calls, ATM method, objection handling |
| Success Stories | 10 min | 2-3 team members share a win from the week |
| Action Items | 5 min | Everyone states their #1 priority for the coming week |

**Business Opportunity Presentation — BOP Format (45 minutes)**

| Segment | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Hook | 5 min | Personal story — your "why" and results |
| Product Story | 10 min | Live demo/sample or video testimonials — let the product sell itself |
| Compensation Plan | 15 min | Rank ladder, Hat Trick/All-Star path, income examples |
| Close and Q&A | 15 min | Call to action — kit selection, enrollment, next steps |

**Demo/Sample Day Format (2 hours)**

| Station | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Open Demos/Samples | 45 min | Hands-on product demos/samples with guests — Liberty, Freedom, Focus, Victory |
| 3-Way Call Station | 30 min | Live 3-way calls with upline for undecided guests |
| Kit Selection | 15 min | Walk guests through Quick Kit, PanAm, Omega, Omni, Momentum |
| Enrollment Station | 30 min | Assisted sign-up, SmartShip setup, First 48 Hours kickoff |
