## Part 1: Why the First 48 Hours Matter

| Principle | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Strike While Hot** | Enthusiasm peaks at commitment. Act fast. |
| **Build Belief** | Early wins create confidence. |

## Part 2: The First 48 Hours Protocol

### Hour 0-2: Lock In the Commitment

| Action | Script/Details | Done? |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **Welcome call/message** | "Congratulations! You made a great decision. Here's what happens next..." | ☐ |
| **Confirm contact info** | Email, phone, best time to reach | ☐ |
| **Set expectations** | "In the next 48 hours, we'll [specific actions]" | ☐ |
| **Schedule first interaction** | Call, meeting, or onboarding session | ☐ |

### Hour 2-12: Reduce Buyer's Remorse

| Action | Script/Details | Done? |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **Send onboarding materials** | Quick start guide, login info, resources | ☐ |
| **Welcome email sequence** | Automated 3-email sequence | ☐ |
| **Social proof delivery** | Success stories, testimonials | ☐ |
| **Introduce to community** | Slack, Facebook group, forum | ☐ |

### Hour 12-24: Mine Their Network (Taprooting)

| Action | Script/Details | Done? |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **Identify top 5 connections** | "Who else do you know who might benefit?" | ☐ |
| **Offer to help introduce** | "Would you be open to a 3-way call?" | ☐ |
| **Referral incentive** | Explain any referral bonuses | ☐ |
| **Request testimonial** | "What made you decide to join?" | ☐ |

### Hour 24-48: Secure a Quick Win

| Action | Script/Details | Done? |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **First result** | Help them achieve one small win | ☐ |
| **Celebrate publicly** | Recognize their win in community | ☐ |
| **Document the win** | Screenshot, quote for future use | ☐ |
| **Set next milestone** | "Now let's work toward [next goal]" | ☐ |

| Mistake | Solution |

| Not celebrating wins | Create recognition system |
