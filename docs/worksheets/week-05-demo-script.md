## Part 1: The D.E.M.O. Framework

Your demo should follow this 4-step structure:

| Step | Action | Duration | Purpose |
|------|--------|----------|---------|
| **D - Demonstrate** | Show the "before" state (the problem). | 10 sec | Establish the pain point visibly. |
| **E - Explain** | Briefly explain what you'll change. | 5 sec | Build anticipation. |
| **M - Magnify** | Amplify the impact of the change. | 10 sec | Show the "after" state (the solution). |
| **O - Overcome** | Address the unspoken objection. | 5 sec | Remove friction to purchase. |

## Part 4: Demo Variations by Business Type

### If you sell a PRODUCT:

| Demo Element | Example |
|--------------|---------|
| **Before state** | Show the problem physically (messy, broken, slow). |
| **Intervention** | Apply/use the product. |
| **After state** | Show the immediate, visible improvement. |
| **Overcome** | "And it only takes 30 seconds / costs $X / lasts forever." |

### If you sell a SERVICE:

| Demo Element | Example |
|--------------|---------|
| **Before state** | Show a real example of bad work (their website, their funnel, their process). |
| **Intervention** | "Let me show you what we'd change." |
| **After state** | Show a mock-up, live edit, or case study result. |
| **Overcome** | "And we can have this done in 48 hours / guaranteed." |

### If you sell COACHING/CONSULTING:

| Demo Element | Example |
|--------------|---------|
| **Before state** | Quick assessment: "Answer these 3 questions..." |
| **Intervention** | "Based on your answers, here's your #1 bottleneck." |
| **After state** | "If we fix this, here's what happens to your revenue." |
| **Overcome** | "And I can show you exactly how in our first session." |

## Part 5: The "Push Test" Equivalent

### What is YOUR "Push Test"?

The Super Patch "Push Test" works because it's:
- **Physical:** You can see and feel the result.
- **Immediate:** Takes <30 seconds.
- **Undeniable:** The subject's own body is the proof.
- **Repeatable:** Anyone can do it.

| Criteria | Your Demo |
|----------|-----------|
| Is it PHYSICAL or VISUAL? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Is it IMMEDIATE (<30 sec)? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Is it UNDENIABLE (hard to argue with)? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Is it REPEATABLE (anyone can do it)? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
