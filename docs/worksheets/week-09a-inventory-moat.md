## Part 1: What is an Inventory Moat?

### Definition
An **Inventory Moat** is when your supply chain, fulfillment, or operational infrastructure creates a competitive advantage that others cannot easily replicate.

### Examples:

| Company | Inventory Moat |
|---------|----------------|
| **Amazon** | Same-day delivery network (warehouses everywhere) |
| **Super Patch** | European warehouses (2-day shipping vs. 10+ days) |
| **Costco** | Bulk buying power + direct relationships |
| **Zara** | Vertical integration (design to store in 2 weeks) |
