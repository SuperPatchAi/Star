## Part 1: Product vs. Platform Definitions

| Attribute | Product Company | Platform Company |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Focus** | Single SKU/service | Multiple offerings on shared foundation |
| **Revenue** | Linear (sell more units) | Compounding (ecosystem effects) |
| **Moat** | Features (easily copied) | Infrastructure (hard to replicate) |
| **Exit Multiple** | 2-4x revenue | 5-10x+ revenue |
| **Example** | A consulting service | A consulting methodology + tools + community |
