## Bonus: CEO's Log Examples

### Example 1: The A/B Decision

> **The day I almost overthought a $500 decision.**
>
> Last week, I spent 3 hours deciding whether to upgrade our email software. The cost? $500/year. 
>
> My CFO finally said: "Is this reversible?" I said yes. She said: "Then why are we still talking?"
>
> I was treating a B Decision (reversible) like an A Decision (irreversible). 
>
> We upgraded. It took 10 minutes to set up. If it doesn't work, we cancel.
>
> **Lesson:** Stop overthinking reversible decisions. The cost of delay often exceeds the cost of being wrong.

### Example 2: The Trust Gap

> **Why our conversion rate dropped 40% last month.**
>
> We changed our landing page. New design. Modern. Beautiful. 
>
> Conversions dropped 40%.
>
> I ran the Trust Audit. Turns out, we removed the customer testimonials to make room for more features. 
>
> We had optimized for "Trust in Vehicle" (our product) and destroyed "Trust in You" (social proof).
>
> We added the testimonials back. Conversions recovered in 48 hours.
>
> **Lesson:** Never sacrifice social proof for aesthetics. Trust beats design.
