## Part 5: Interview Questions by Competency

### Track Record
| Question | What to Listen For |
|----------|-------------------|
| "Walk me through your biggest professional achievement." | Specific metrics, their role, impact |
| "Tell me about a time you exceeded expectations." | Evidence of going above and beyond |
| "What results are you most proud of?" | Quantifiable outcomes |

### Velocity
| Question | What to Listen For |
|----------|-------------------|
| "Describe a project you completed faster than expected." | Efficiency, initiative |
| "How do you prioritize when everything is urgent?" | Decision-making speed |
| "What's your approach when given a tight deadline?" | Resourcefulness |

### Ownership
| Question | What to Listen For |
|----------|-------------------|
| "Tell me about a failure and how you handled it." | Accountability, no blame |
| "Describe a time you took initiative without being asked." | Proactive behavior |
| "How do you handle a situation where you disagree with leadership?" | Constructive ownership |

### Culture Add
| Question | What to Listen For |
|----------|-------------------|
| "How do you typically contribute to team dynamics?" | Energy, collaboration |
| "Describe your ideal work environment." | Alignment with your culture |
| "Tell me about the best team you've been part of." | What they value |

### Coachability
| Question | What to Listen For |
|----------|-------------------|
| "Tell me about feedback that changed how you work." | Receptivity, growth |
| "How do you typically seek improvement?" | Self-awareness |
| "Describe a time you were wrong and how you handled it." | Humility |
