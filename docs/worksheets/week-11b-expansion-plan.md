## Part 7: Risk Triggers & Response

### Early Warning Signs:

| Trigger | Threshold | Response |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| CAC too high | >2x plan | Pause paid, optimize |
| Low conversion | <1% | Review funnel, localization |
| Operational failures | >10% orders | Fix ops, pause marketing |
| Regulatory issue | Any | Stop, consult legal |

### Escalation Path:

| Issue Level | Action | Who Decides |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| Minor (tactical) | Local team fixes | Country lead |
| Major (strategic) | Pause and review | CEO + Country lead |
| Critical (existential) | Exit discussion | Board/CEO |
