## Part 1: What is the CapEx Model?

### Definition
The **CapEx Model** is a crowd-funded advertising system where customers, partners, or distributors contribute funds to a collective marketing pool, which is then used to run campaigns that benefit all participants.

### How Super Patch Does It:
1. Distributors purchase "CapEx tokens" ($50-$500)
2. Funds are pooled into a central advertising budget
3. Company runs campaigns (TV, billboards, social media)
4. Leads generated are distributed back to token holders
5. Result: $5M+ in advertising funded without corporate capital

## Part 5: Lead Distribution System

### How will leads be distributed?

| Method | Description | Pros | Cons |
|--------|-------------|------|------|
| **Pro-rata** | Based on contribution size | Fair, predictable | Complex tracking |
| **Round-robin** | Equal distribution, sequential | Simple | Ignores contribution level |
| **Territory-based** | Geographic exclusivity | Clear ownership | Limits scale |
| **First-come** | Speed-based | Rewards engagement | Can feel unfair |
