# Interview Question Bank
**The $100M Blueprint — Week 8 Worksheet**

---

## How to Use This Document

1. Select 3-5 questions per competency for each interview stage
2. Ask the same questions to all candidates (consistency)
3. Take detailed notes
4. Score each answer 1-10
5. Compare candidates objectively

---

## Stage 1: Screening Questions (30 min)

### Opening Questions

| Question | Purpose | Score (1-10) |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| "Walk me through your career journey. What led you here?" | Narrative, self-awareness | /10 |
| "Why are you interested in this role specifically?" | Motivation, research | /10 |
| "What do you know about our company?" | Preparation, interest | /10 |
| "What are you looking for in your next role?" | Alignment, expectations | /10 |

### Quick Competency Check

| Question | Assesses | Score (1-10) |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| "What's your biggest professional achievement in the last 2 years?" | Track Record | /10 |
| "How do you typically approach a new project?" | Velocity, Ownership | /10 |
| "What would your current/last manager say is your superpower?" | Self-awareness | /10 |
| "What would they say you could improve?" | Coachability | /10 |

### Red Flag Questions

| Question | Red Flag to Watch For |
|----------|----------------------|
| "Why did you leave your last role?" | Blame, negativity, no ownership |
| "Tell me about a conflict with a coworker." | Inability to resolve, blames others |
| "What's your biggest weakness?" | "I work too hard" (cliché, no self-awareness) |

## Stage 2: Competency Deep-Dive (60 min)

### Track Record Questions

| Question | What to Listen For | Score |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| "Tell me about your most significant accomplishment. Walk me through it in detail." | Specifics, metrics, their role | /10 |
| "What's a goal you set for yourself and achieved?" | Initiative, follow-through | /10 |
| "Describe a project where you exceeded expectations." | Going above and beyond | /10 |
| "What metrics do you use to measure your success?" | Data-driven mindset | /10 |
| "What's the impact of your work in dollars/users/efficiency?" | Quantifiable results | /10 |

### Velocity Questions

| Question | What to Listen For | Score |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| "Describe a time you had to move fast. What did you do?" | Speed without sacrificing quality | /10 |
| "How do you prioritize when you have 10 things to do?" | Decision-making framework | /10 |
| "Tell me about a shortcut you took that worked." | Smart efficiency | /10 |
| "What's your approach to getting unstuck?" | Resourcefulness | /10 |
| "How do you balance speed with quality?" | Trade-off awareness | /10 |

### Ownership Questions

| Question | What to Listen For | Score |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| "Tell me about a time something went wrong. What happened?" | Accountability, no blame | /10 |
| "Describe a situation where you had to make a decision without all the information." | Bias for action | /10 |
| "How do you handle a situation where you disagree with your manager?" | Constructive pushback | /10 |
| "Tell me about a time you took initiative without being asked." | Proactive behavior | /10 |
| "What do you own in your current role that you created?" | Building, not just executing | /10 |

### Culture Add Questions

| Question | What to Listen For | Score |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| "What energizes you at work?" | Alignment with your culture | /10 |
| "Describe your ideal team environment." | Collaboration style | /10 |
| "How do you typically contribute to team dynamics?" | Positive energy | /10 |
| "Tell me about the best team you've been part of. Why?" | What they value | /10 |
| "How do you handle working with someone you don't click with?" | Maturity, professionalism | /10 |

### Coachability Questions

| Question | What to Listen For | Score |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| "Tell me about feedback that changed how you work." | Receptivity to feedback | /10 |
| "What's something you've learned recently?" | Continuous learning | /10 |
| "Describe a time you were wrong and how you handled it." | Humility | /10 |
| "How do you typically seek improvement?" | Self-driven growth | /10 |
| "What would you do differently in your last role?" | Reflection, learning | /10 |

## Stage 3: Case Study / Work Sample (60-90 min)

### Case Study Format

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **The Problem** | Present a real business challenge from your company |
| **Time Limit** | 30 min prep + 30 min presentation |
| **What to Provide** | Context, data, constraints |
| **What to Assess** | Thinking process, creativity, communication |

### Case Study Scoring

| Criteria | Weight | Score (1-10) | Weighted |
|----------|--------|--------------|----------|
| **Problem Understanding** | 15% | /10 | |
| **Analysis Quality** | 25% | /10 | |
| **Solution Creativity** | 20% | /10 | |
| **Communication Clarity** | 20% | /10 | |
| **Q&A Performance** | 20% | /10 | |
| **TOTAL** | 100% | | /100 |

### Sample Case Study Prompts by Role

**Marketing:**
> "Our customer acquisition cost increased 40% last quarter. Here's our data. How would you diagnose and fix this?"

**Sales:**
> "Our conversion rate from demo to close dropped from 30% to 20%. Walk me through how you'd investigate and improve this."

**Operations:**
> "We're planning to expand to 3 new markets this year. How would you prioritize and plan for this?"

**Product:**
> "Our users are requesting 20 different features. We can only build 3 this quarter. How would you decide?"

## Stage 4: Board Room / Final Interview (45-60 min)

### Executive Presence Questions

| Question | What to Assess | Score |
|----------|----------------|-------|
| "If you joined and had 90 days to prove yourself, what would you do?" | Strategy, prioritization | /10 |
| "What questions do you have for me about the business?" | Curiosity, depth | /10 |
| "What would make you leave this job in 2 years?" | Self-awareness, expectations | /10 |
| "How do you handle stress and pressure?" | Resilience | /10 |
| "What's your leadership philosophy?" | Values, style | /10 |

### Stress Test Questions

| Question | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| "I'm not sure you have enough experience for this role. Change my mind." | Handle pushback |
| "Your case study solution has a flaw. [Point it out.] What do you think?" | Coachability under pressure |
| "If I called your last 3 managers, what would they say?" | Consistency, honesty |

### Closing Questions

| Question | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| "What concerns do you have about this role?" | Surface objections |
| "What would it take for you to say yes?" | Understand priorities |
| "Is there anything you want us to know that we haven't covered?" | Final chance to impress |
