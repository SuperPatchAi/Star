## Part 1: The Talent Density Philosophy

**Core Principle:** "One A-Player = Three B-Players"

| Player Type | Definition | Characteristics |
|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| **A-Player** | Top 10% performer. Raises the bar. | Self-directed, high output, culture add. |
| **B-Player** | Solid performer. Meets expectations. | Reliable, needs guidance, maintains status quo. |
| **C-Player** | Below expectations. Drags team down. | Requires hand-holding, misses targets, culture drain. |

**Target:** 80%+ A-Players on your team.
