## Part 1: The 3 Trust Factors

Every sale, partnership, or commitment depends on THREE types of trust:

| Factor | Definition | Question It Answers |
|--------|------------|---------------------|
| **Trust in YOU** | Belief in your authenticity, expertise, and intent. | "Can I trust this person?" |
| **Trust in VEHICLE** | Belief in your product, service, or company. | "Does this thing actually work?" |
| **Trust in SELF** | Belief in their own ability to succeed with your offer. | "Can I actually do this?" |

**Key Insight:** Most sales fail not because of trust in you or your product, but because the prospect doesn't believe THEY can succeed.
