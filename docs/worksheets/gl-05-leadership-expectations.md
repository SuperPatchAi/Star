## Part 2: Leader IPA Standards

The 80/15/5 Rule defines where your time goes: **80% Level 1** (demos/samples, inviting, taprooting), **15% Level 2** (nurturing), **5% Level 3** (training). Leaders who flip this ratio stall.

### Daily Non-Negotiables

These are the activities you complete every single day — no exceptions, no excuses.

- ☐ Self-development (20–30 minutes: book, podcast, training replay)
- ☐ Social media engagement — ATM method (Add, Tag, Message)
- ☐ Minimum 2 demos/samples or invitations
- ☐ Follow-up on all outstanding prospects
- ☐ At least 1 three-way call (give or receive)
- ☐ Check in with 2 team members
- ☐ Recognize 1 team win publicly

### Weekly Non-Negotiables

- ☐ Host or attend team call
- ☐ Onboard at least 1 new associate (execute First 48 Hours Protocol)
- ☐ Review team TGQV and leg balance (60% rule check)
- ☐ Plan next week's demo/sample schedule
- ☐ Conduct at least 1 one-on-one coaching session with an emerging leader

### Monthly Non-Negotiables

- ☐ Review SmartShip retention rates across your organization
- ☐ Host or attend 1 live event (Demo/Sample Day, BOP, or regional)
- ☐ Evaluate rank advancement progress against quarterly milestones
- ☐ Update prospect pipeline — remove stale contacts, add new ones
- ☐ Team recognition and awards (public shout-outs, incentive prizes)
- ☐ CapEx token investment review — evaluate lead flow and ROI
