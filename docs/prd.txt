1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We are launching a high-ticket business coaching business leveraged on the personal brand and operational success of Jay Dhaliwal (CEO, The Super Patch Company). The goal is to attract high-net-worth individuals, executives, and entrepreneurs seeking to build generational wealth and scalable assets.

2. OBJECTIVES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Establish a premium brand identity centered on "The Operator's Coach" (Active CEO vs. Theorist).
- Launch a "Founder's Cohort" (Beta group) to validate curriculum and generate case studies.
- Build a high-ticket sales funnel (Lead Magnet -> Application -> Discovery Call).
- Achieve $1M in revenue within the first 12 months.

3. TARGET AUDIENCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNWIs)
- C-Level Executives
- Established Entrepreneurs looking to scale
- Niche: Health, Wellness, Tech, Direct Sales leaders

4. PRODUCT / OFFERING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- **Format:** Hybrid Model
    - 1-on-1 Mentorship (Premium Tier)
    - Small Group Cohorts (Core Tier)
    - Digital Assets/SOPs (Foundation)
- **Unique Value Proposition (UVP):** 
    - Proven frameworks from The Super Patch Company ($0 to $100M+ in 2 years).
    - Focus on "Generational Wealth" and "Seeing Around the Corner."
    - Drug-free/Wellness-centric business philosophy.
- **Pricing:** High Ticket ($10k - $50k+ packages).

5. KEY WORKSTREAMS & TASKS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    - Define specific "Founder's Offer" pricing and deliverables.
    - Create the "Jay Dhaliwal" coaching brand guideline (distinct from but aligned with Super Patch).
    - Competitor benchmarking (Tony Robbins, Grant Cardone, but for the "Quiet Professional").

5.2. Marketing & Lead Gen

    - Build the Lead Magnet (e.g., "The $100M Blueprint" whitepaper/video).
    - Set up the email marketing automation (ActiveCampaign/HubSpot).

5.3. Sales & Operations
    - Create the High-Ticket Application Form (Qualifying questions).
    - Develop the Sales Script/Framework for Discovery Calls.
    - Select and set up the hosting platform (Circle.so, Kajabi, or GoHighLevel).

5.4. Curriculum Development
    - Outline the 12-week "Generational Wealth" syllabus.
    - Extract key frameworks from Jay's current operations (e.g., VTT product launch, CapEx program).
    - Create initial worksheets and decision matrices.

6. SUCCESS METRICS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Number of qualified applications per week.
- Conversion rate from Discovery Call to Sale (Target: 20%+).
