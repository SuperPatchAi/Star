## Form Logic / Conditional Rules

### Disqualification Logic

**Auto-decline if:**
- Q14 (Investment) = "No, this is outside my budget"
- Q20 (Hours) = "Less than 5 hours" AND Q23 (Commitment) < 7

**Show custom message:**
> "Thank you for your interest. Based on your responses, this program may not be the right fit at this time. We encourage you to revisit when your circumstances change."

### Fast-Track Logic

**Priority review if:**
- Q12 (Revenue) = "$1M+" AND Q14 (Investment) = "Yes"
- Q23 (Commitment) = 9 or 10
