## FAQs

### Program FAQs

**Q: How much time per week is required?**
> A: Plan for 5-7 hours/week: 90-min session + 2-3 hours of pre/post work + community engagement.

**Q: What if I miss a live session?**
> A: All sessions are recorded. You must watch within 48 hours to stay on track.

**Q: Is this program right for my industry?**
> A: The frameworks are universal. We've had success with SaaS, DTC, services, MLM, coaching, and more.

**Q: Can I get 1-on-1 time with Jay beyond the Hot Seats?**
> A: Founding Members get Voxer access for quick questions. Extended 1-on-1 is available separately.

**Q: What happens after the 12 weeks?**
> A: You get lifetime access to materials, alumni community, and a 90-day check-in call.

### Investment FAQs

**Q: Can I expense this to my business?**
> A: Yes. This is a legitimate business education expense. Consult your accountant.

**Q: What's your refund policy?**

**Q: Is the investment tax-deductible?**
